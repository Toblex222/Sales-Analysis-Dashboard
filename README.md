# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard
An interactive Microsoft Excel Sales Dashboard designed to monitor sales performance across regions, territories, brands, and daily sales trends. Built using Microsoft Excel, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Excel formulas, the dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights.
## 📌 Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance through interactive visualizations and dynamic filters. Users can analyze sales by region, territory, and brand while tracking daily sales trends and key performance indicators to support better business decisions.

## 📈 Key Insights
The business recorded 1,052 total sales, comprising 954 retail sales and 98 wholesale sales, indicating that retail sales contribute over 90% of total transactions.
The average daily sales stand at 35, providing a benchmark for monitoring daily business performance.
Central Region generated the highest sales with 431 transactions (41%), making it the strongest-performing region.
Southern Region followed with 310 sales (29%), while North Region contributed 262 sales (25%). South Region recorded the lowest sales at 49 transactions (5%), presenting an opportunity for market expansion.
MiloBite emerged as the best-selling brand with 376 sales, closely followed by Crusto (346 sales). Together, these two brands account for a significant share of total sales.
Several brands, including MochaPop, recorded little or no sales, indicating products that may require promotional campaigns or inventory review.
Daily sales fluctuated throughout the month, with the highest sales of 74 recorded on Day 30, while Days 22 and 23 recorded zero sales, highlighting periods of unusually low business activity.
